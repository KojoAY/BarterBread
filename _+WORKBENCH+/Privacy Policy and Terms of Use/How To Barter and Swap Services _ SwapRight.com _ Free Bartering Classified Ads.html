What is "Login with Facebook"
Facebook introduced "Facebook for Websites" as a way for Facebook users to easily interact with 3rd-party websites without needing to create a seperate account. We want to ensure you we fully respect your privacy, and offer this feature as a simple way to post swap offers on SwapRight. If you prefer to create a seperate SwapRight account, you can do so here.

Facebook provides us only with the information available in your public profile as well as an anonymous email address.

The public information displayed on SwapRight from your Facebook Profile:
  • First Name, Last Initial
  • State or Region and Country
  • Profile Picture (you can remove/change your picture used on SwapRight once logged in)
Q: Do you capture my Facebook password?
A: No, the pop-up you enter your password into is served by Facebook, we have no access to that information.
Q: Will you send messages to my Facebook friends?
A: No, we will never email or send messages to your Facebook friends unless you specifically ask us to invite them.
Q: How do I cancel my SwapRight account after using Facebook to login?
A: In Facebook, go to Account > Application Settings and simply remove the 'SwapRight' application. All your posts and information will be removed from our website within 24 hours.
Q: Do I have to login using Facebook?
A: No, we just provide that as a convenient option. You can create a seperate SwapRight account here.

Frequently Asked Questions
  • Is SwapRight free?
    Yes, SwapRight is 100% free. You can put away your credit card.
  • Can I swap products on SwapRight or just services?
    SwapRight focuses primarily on the exchange of services, although it is OK to swap products on SwapRight as long as a service is included in the swap offer. If you are only looking to swap a product for a product, then that goes against the spirit of SwapRight.

    We're new, so as we get feedback from users we may adjust this policy. If you have any input regarding this issue, please let us know your thoughts using the 'feedback' tab on the left side of the website.

  • What if the service I am offering is different in value from the service I need?
    This is a common issue, the best idea is to just get creative. Maybe offer a few more hours of service, or you can always offset any difference with cash or something else.
  • When I contact somebody about their swap offer do they see my email address?
    No, when you contact somebody about their swap offer, the message is sent through SwapRight and does not include your email address.

    If they are interested, they can reply to your message with their email, so that future correspondence can take place outside of SwapRight.

  • I sent somebody a message and never got a reply.
    First you can check Your Account to see if they have replied to you. We send all replies to you via email, but spam filters can block these messages, so we also include a copy for you in Your Account.

    If they didn't reply, it probably just means they weren't interested. Don't be discouraged, new swap offers are posted all the time.

  • Is SwapRight International?
    Yes, you can use SwapRight in any country where law permits. Currently SwapRight is an English-only website. If we get some growth we hope to expand to support other languages.
  • What's the difference between swapping, trading and bartering?
    People tend to use the terms interchangeably, I'm sure there are some technical differences, but we just like the sound of 'Swap' better than 'Trade' or 'Barter'. Also all the good domain names with 'Trade' in them were already taken.
  • Still have questions?
    Please contact us and we'll do our best to answer your questions.
